Project Proposal:
Balls 2 the Wall (Business Concept for Nutritional Snack Balls)

  • What is your website about?

    • I plan to launch my website about a business I hope to start one day. In a way, I hope to create a website which could be representative of one that could be used as the company begins production. Attached is a snippet of the executive summary that I created:


      "Balls2theWall Food Inc. is a company that aspires to become a dominant player in the healthy food snack market. B2W aims to facilitate excitement around maintaining a healthy lifestyle for individuals who are frequently on the move. B2W offers nutritional energy balls which are delicious, nutritious, unique, and do not require TCS (time and temperature control for safety) .

  • What is the objective of your website? (Also read as why would this site be important?)

    • My personal objective is to learn how to properly create functional/visually appealing website for a business/start-up. As I recently found out, most marketers believe that their website is the channel that results in the greatest ROI. Given, I feel like it is essential to have an understanding for what is going into a channel that is so important to the success of a business. In general, I think this site would be important because B2W hopes to hold up its high standards for CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility). In doing this, sustainability with regards to packaging and an efficient supply chain which works to limit food waste and improper treatment of factory workers/farmers. Additionally, giving back to the community (through charitable causes) will be an aspect that is crucial to the intended purpose of the company; thus, this website will be important because it will aim to establish the standard of what a modern day company should truly look like.

  • Who is your target audience?

    • Balls2TheWall will aim to focus their marketing efforts on health-conscious,trendy,sustainability-driven individuals who are constantly on the move. The target audience will age will range from approximately 25 to 40 year olds. Moreover, given the high quality of ingredients in such snack balls, the target market will need to have the appropriate amount of disposable income to afford a snack with a slightly elevated price (in comparison to competition who may compromise quality).

  • What content do you plan to include in each page of your website? (Note: some of these tabs will need to contain a decent amount of hypothetical information since the business is not currently in operation
    1. Product Lines (Breaking down different product lines of snack balls the company plans to have)
    2. How to Buy (Online purchasing options/ Brick-and-Mortar Locations/What retail stores the snacks are sold in)
    3. Mission/Sustainability (Planet / Society/Charitable causes)
    4. News (Future Plans/Plans for expansion/News stories relevant to company)
    5. Suppliers (Communicating transparency with regards to sourcing of ingredients)
